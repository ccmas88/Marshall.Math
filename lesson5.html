Worked Example

Problem: Simplify \(2^3 \times 2^4\)

Step 1: Use the property \(a^m \times a^n = a^{m+n}\)

Step 2: Add the exponents: \(3 + 4 = 7\)

Step 3: Write the result: \(2^7 = 128\)
